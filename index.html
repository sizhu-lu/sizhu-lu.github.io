Welcome to my homepage!

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Statistics, UC Berkeley. I am very fortunate to be advised by Professor Peng Ding. I am also very fortunate to be advised by Professor Ting Ye on some projects. Previously, I received my B.S. in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics and B.A. in Finance from Peking University.
